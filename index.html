About Me

Hello my name is Cynthia and I like to take pictures. I'm just a photographer learning to take pictures of cool things that facinate me. I am 22 years old and I have been focusing on many aspects of photography. From nightskys to hot Summer days, I have always had an interest on taking pictures of nature's beauty and what it has to offer. I have always been facinated with the many wonders of the world many seem to pass by everyday. Look at my gallery and see through my eyes through the lens of my camera.
